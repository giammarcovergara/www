Vernis Luxor D1P

patent leather

A calfskin leather coated through a specific process that gives it a glossy effect.

specificities

Hand: very structured,stable year after year. Grain: smooth and covered. Touch: polished.

client's benefits

Keeps its structure throughout the years. Water repellent.

care istructions

Very sensitive to scratches. Avoid extended contact with colored materials that may transfer on it, or with patent leathers, that may stick on it. Keep it away from any acid or greasy product, cosmetics or solvent. Clean with a soft, dry, light-colored cloth. Dry in the shade. Store it in the protective pouch provided when not in use.

Raso/RSO

patent leather

A carefully selected silk fiber fabric (thread dyed) worked with traditional methods from the Middle Age.

specificities

Hand: extremely thin, supple and light, satin is reinforced on leather goods to give structure to the product. Touch: smooth and silky.

client's benefits

Luxurious fabric, delicate and sensual. The ultimate femininity for an elegant look.

care istructions

Avoid any contact with abrasive surfaces to prevent the product from unthreading. Keep it away from greasy substances, cosmetics or any product that can stain it. In case of stain, gently dab with a soft cloth dampened with water. Never use solvent. No dry cleaning. Store it in the protective pouch provided when not in use.

Suede leather

The digital print technic

A digital print of colors directly into the leather.
Step 1: the leather crust is first treated as usual to reach the appropriate suppleness.
Step 2: the digital image is reverse-printed on a film and the film is laminated to leather with a press.
Step 3: the leather is finished just like other leathers.

client's beneficts

A pattern totally printed into the leather and that connot be rubbled from it.

Possible leathers

Patent leather: Vernis Luxor. The layer of patent is applied directly on the printing. Nappa leather: A very natural supple leather that let all the natural specificities of the skin visible. The highest level of expertise to print on such a leather.
