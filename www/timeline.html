2013 Virgule, ETC. In the Footsteps of Roger Vivier at Palais de Tokyo, from 2nd october to 18th November 2013

2013 The Red Cross’ “Petite Enfance” program, the Fabrique des sens (Sensory Factory) project has helped develop a sensorial kit to encourage child development through artistic and cultural discovery. More than one thousand children in day care centers have benefited from this program.

2013 Discover the exhibition Virgule, etc. in the footsteps of Roger Vivier with Ines de la Fressange, Bruno Frisoni and Olivier Saillard.

2013 With texts by Olivier Saillard, Virginie Mouzat, Colombe Pringle, Loïc Prigent, Elizabeth Sammelhack and Sophie Fontanel and conversations between Ines de la Fressange and Catherine Deneuve, and Bruno Frisoni with Cate Blanchett.

2013 Created by Roger Vivier in 1963, under his own name, this historical Comma heel resembles an exclamation mark: vibrant, appealing, a fusion of daring and sophistication.First seen in the 2013/2014 Limited Edition Rendez-Vous Autumn-Winter collection, Bruno Frisoni infuses it. Revitalised in a higher and edgier contemporary shape, the Virgule steals the stage as a new Sexy Cult collection classic.

2012 Jewels in the perfumery’s crown, five fragrances showcase five exquisite ingredients: L’Iris, Le Néroli, La Rose, Le Santal, L’Ambre

2012 The Maison dresses Queen Silvia and Crown Princess Victoria of Sweden for her wedding ceremony, the 21st of June

2012 Roger Vivier dresses Cate Blanchett in her role as Maid Marion in Ridley Scott’s Robin Hood

2012 Roger Vivier presents Rendez-Vous, Limited Edition collection for the Spring-Summer 2011. A fusion, a channel between Couture’s expertise in excellence and the contemporary vibrancy of prêt-à-porter.

2011 Catherine Deneuve, Chiara Mastrioani, Ludivine Sagnier wearing Roger Vivier in the movie Les Bien-Aimés by Christophe Honoré.

2011 In November, Auction the Roger Vivier Brand bought archives pieces. This shoe creatd by Roger Vivier in 1962 for Princess Soraya, the beautiful “sad Princess”, second wife of the Shah of Iran. In its most recent sale, the single existing shoe beat the estimate auction price by miles, reaching the record-breaking figure of 19.763 euros for one single shoe!
